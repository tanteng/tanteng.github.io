window.location.assign和window.location.href区别

Jun 12, 2015 in DEVELOP • 前端
1 min read

window.location.assign(url) 和 window.location.href=url 实现功能是一样的，都是跳转到网址，只是用法稍微不同。

window.location.assign(url); window.location = url; window.location.href = url;

最大的不同是，assign 会添加记录到浏览历史，点击后退可以返回之前页面。

They simply navigate to the new URL. The replace method on the other hand navigates to the URL without adding a new record to the history.

So, what you have read in those many forms is not correct. The assign method does add a new record to the history.
